About Our Store

Preeti Collection, also known as Menika Vastralya, is a trusted name in the fashion retail industry located in Maulapur-5, Rautahat. We have been serving our valued customers with high-quality branded readymade clothing for over a decade.

Our store specializes in providing fashionable and comfortable clothing options for the entire family - men, women, and children. We carefully curate our collection to ensure we offer the latest trends while maintaining traditional values in our designs.

At Preeti Collection, we believe that everyone deserves to look their best without compromising on quality or breaking the bank. Our friendly staff is always ready to help you find the perfect outfit for any occasion.
